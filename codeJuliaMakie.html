Beautiful Makie

Please go to the new Beautiful Makie web page , there everything is self contained.


The goal here for me is to have in one place things that I will need or I already need on a daily basis for my work. And as a byproduct I hope this helps others with easy copy-paste code so that they can obtain easy and quick figures.
Makie is a high-performance, extendable, and multi-platform plotting ecosystem for the Julia programming language. From the japanese word Maki-e, which is a technique to sprinkle lacquer with gold and silver powder. Data is basically the gold and silver of our age, so let's spread it out beautifully on the screen!
Before going into the examples and running them please download the makieThemes files or download the folder with all examples.
